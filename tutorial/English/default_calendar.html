Default Chinese Calendar in -721–2200

The default calendar supports years from -721 to 2200. There were certain periods in the past when China was divided into many states, and multiple verions of Chinese calendars were used in different states in those periods. The default Chinese calendar chooses a specific set of calendars in those periods. We follow the choice in 3500 Years of Calendars and Astronomical PhenomenaZhang97. The calendar data in -103 – 2200 are stored in an array for table lookup. The Lu calendar in -721 – -480 is reconstructed based on the information in Section 3.5 of Ancient Chinese Calendars and AlmanacsZCBH. The computation method is explained on my Chunqiu Calendar page. The Zhou calendar, which is one of the ancient six calendars, in -479 – -221 is computed using the equations described on my ancient six calendar page. The Qin and Early Han calendar in -220 – -104 is computed using the equations described on my Qin-Early Han calendar page.
