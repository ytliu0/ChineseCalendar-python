In the code, Chunqiu, LiaoJinYuan and SouthernMing calendar data are obtained by table lookup. Thus, they can only be used in the years of usage. The other calendar data (apart from GBT) are calculated and can in principle be used outside the years of usage, but it is strongly discouraged to do so. The calendar data for GBT are computed based on the pre-computed DE431/DE441 Moon phase and solar term data in -3500 – 3500. However, the GBT calendar data before 1929 are known to deviate from the actual calendar, and the computed GBT data in far future are subjected to the uncertainty of Earth's irregular rotation rate.
